About Me

Mopati has ten years of experience in the commercial management and administration of construction projects. In that time he has represented the commercial interests of both contractors and principals, this experience has provided Mopati with a balance of knowledge and information which he uses to ensure the correct commercial outcome is acheived in all given situation.
Mopati’s strong commitment to achieving the best commercial outcome has allowed him to develop strong negotiation skills and a detailed knowledge of the technical aspects of construction and building services.
Mopati operates efficiently and productively within a project team and his enthusiastic personality and perseverance ensures that he gets the most from those working with him. Mopati’s experience in live construction projects ensures that he can operate to tight deadlines and understands the key functions required to successfully deliver the commercial aspects of a contract.
